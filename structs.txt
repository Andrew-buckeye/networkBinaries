There are a ton of structs, here is a cheat sheet.
https://man7.org/linux/man-pages/


Addresses
    sockaddr, generic address structure 
    sockaddr_in, IPv4 address structure
        in_addr, Actual IPv4 address
    sockaddr_in6, IPv6 address structure
        in6_addr, Actual IPv6 address


DNS/host resolution
    hostent, DNS resolution, used by gethostbyname() and gethostbyaddr()
    addrinfo, used by getaddrinfo(), keeps data like Cname, flag, family, socketype, etc

IP/transport headers
    iphdr, 
    tcphdr, 
    udphdr,
    icmphdr

Sockets/options
